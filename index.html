Featured Contributor Insight  ·  Retirement Distribution Strategy

The clients who run out of money in retirement almost never made bad investments. They made good investments — but in the wrong sequence, from the wrong accounts, at the wrong time. Distribution strategy is where retirement plans actually succeed or fail.

Certified State Advisors who specialize in the transition from accumulation to distribution increasingly emphasize that the "what to invest in" question matters far less in retirement than "which account to draw from and when." Tax-efficient withdrawal sequencing — coordinating traditional IRAs, Roth accounts, and taxable brokerage withdrawals to minimize lifetime tax drag — can add years to portfolio longevity without changing a single investment holding.
